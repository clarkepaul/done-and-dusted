Since July 2019

Bought in July 2019, Done and Dusted was bought as a weekend wonderer. I was sold on the ample space of the bus being a wide and high but perhaps one of the biggest selling points were the 4 fully seatbelted seats which meant we could all ride up the front together. Having two small children we wanted forward facing seats with sholder seatbelts. was created with a goal of creating an online community of like-minded people. Our goal is to reduce stress, and live a simpler life.
