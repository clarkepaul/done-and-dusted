The adventure begins

Our bus Done and Dusted was bought as a weekend wonderer in July 2019. We wanted ample space for the kids and living, being extra wide this bus provides plenty. One of the biggest selling points was the seating arrangement with 4 full seatbelts meaning we could all ride up the front together. Hopefully we'll get to spend some good times together, reduce stress by spending more time out and about or within nature.

We bought it fully kitted out with all of the basics we need for the time being.

It came with; kitchen, fridge, oven, gas hot water, toilet, shower, solar and alternator (12v/24v), swivel 22 inch TV, LED lights throughout, stainless grey/fresh tanks (200ltrs), 4 berth with full seatbelts, double bed, seperate dinning area, towbar, large stand up boot.

Its been named Done and Dusted by the previous owners and we might just keep it like that. It's spent most of its life in Auckland but has previously done a stint in Wellington several years ago.

It was a passenger coach up to about 2007-9 (owned by several tour companies), we are the third private use owner but most of the motorhome conversion was done by the previous owner.
