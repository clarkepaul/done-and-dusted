An adventure begins

Done and Dusted was bought as a weekend wonderer in July 2019. We wanted ample space for the kids and living, being extra wide this bus provides plenty. One of the biggest selling points were the 4 fully seatbelted seats which meant we could all ride up the front together. Having two small children we wanted forward facing seats with sholder seatbelts. Our goal is to spend more time together, reduce stress by spending more time in nature.
