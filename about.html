The adventure begins


Done and Dusted was bought as a weekend wonderer in July 2019. We wanted ample space for the kids and living, being extra wide this bus provides plenty. One of the biggest selling points were the 4 fully seatbelted seats which meant we could all ride up the front together. Having two small children we wanted forward facing seats with sholder seatbelts. Our goal is to spend more time together, reduce stress by spending more time in nature.


1980 NZ Built Ford Bus

I think that around 2007-9 it would have been converted to a motorhome. Some of its current features include; toilet and shower, towbar, large stand up boot, swivel 22 inch TV, LED lights through the bus, 12v and 24v, stainless grey waste and fresh tanks (200ltrs), kitchen with fridge and oven with hobs, seatbelts for 4 people but also has a seperate dinning area. 7.2mtrs, 4 birth, power steering, solar and alternator, running hot water, 5 speed manual. Built in New Zealand for our roads (D series, model DOG10). It has a wide body with aluminum cladding. It was converted to a 6 cylinder (6000cc) 6D14 Mitsubishi motor at some stage with a 5 speed gearbox.

It has spent most of its life in Auckland but it did a stint in Wellington so its heading back south.

It was a coach from 1980-2007 (owned by 6 passenger service/tour companies), and we are the third private use owners so the 9th owner all up.
