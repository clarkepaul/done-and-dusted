The adventure begins


Done and Dusted was bought as a weekend wonderer in July 2019. We wanted ample space for the kids and living, being extra wide this bus provides plenty. One of the biggest selling points were the 4 full body seatbelts which meant we could all ride up the front together. Having two small children we wanted forward facing seats with sholder seatbelts. Our goal is to spend more time together, reduce stress by spending more time in nature.

7.2mtr 1980 NZ Built Ford Bus

I think that the bus was converted to a motorhome around 2007-9. Some of its current features include; kitchen, fridge, oven, gas hot water, toilet, shower, towbar, large stand up boot, swivel 22 inch TV, LED lights throughout, stainless grey/fresh tanks (200ltrs), 4 berth with full seatbelts, seperate dinning area, solar and alternator (12v/24v). History and specs

Built in New Zealand (D series, model DOG10) 5 speed manual with power steering. It has a wide body with aluminum cladding. It was converted to a 6 cylinder (6000cc) 6D14 Mitsubishi motor at some stage with a 5 speed gearbox.

It's spent most of its life in Auckland but it do a stint in Wellington, so its heading back there for the time being.

It was a coach from 1980-2007 (owned by 6 passenger service/tour companies), and we are the third private use owners so the 9th owner all up.
