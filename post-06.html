House-bus/truck gathering

Retro bus get together in Woodville.

  • Destination

    Ferry Reserve, Woodville

  • When

    Oct 2019, Labour weekend

  • Company

    Paul and Tui

2019 Woodville Housebus & Truck Happening is the second year it's been organised. It's not organised through any organisation but just by a couple of people through a Facebook group. The gathering seems to have attracted quite some attention and the group has grown rapidly, last year was apparently about 40 vehicles but this year seems it should be at least 80.

People have been at the camp since Tuesday, with all the rain three buses needed to get pulled out of the mud an now they are all parked up on a higher level away from the river.

People are coming from all around New Zealand, its interesting to hear all the updates as people are meeting up on their trips, driving in convoys. By the looks of it ther might be as many as 100 buses/trucks coming.

Woodville tunes

Something I used to do when I was younger was make mix tapes for roadtrips and holidays, bring back the old days I say! Every now and then you'd find the old playlist and it brings back those memories.

I've actually dug out my old iPod fully loaded with tunes from at least 10 years ago, and my bag of mini-discs, some of those mixes are well worth digging back up. They'll plug straight into the stereo system so should be good for the drive.
